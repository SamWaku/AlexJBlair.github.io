About The Artist

A.J. Dimicco is an aspiring artist who has a passion for creating cartoons and digital drawings.
She enjoys making colorful and imaginative works of art in her free time.
As a recent graduate of Whitworth University she hopes to attend graduate school and eventually an art school to pursue her passion.
Many of her works have a unique style but that does not mean she is limited by any means. She is willing to tackle a challenge.
In the gallery feel free to see some of her most prized works. There will also be many out of her style as well.
